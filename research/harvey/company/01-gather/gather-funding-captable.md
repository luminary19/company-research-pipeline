### Per-round conflicts / discrepancies (surfaced, not resolved)

- **Series A amount:** Harvey/Sequoia announce **$21M** [R1]. PitchBook lists Series A at **$25.9M** (raised-to-date $25.9M), dated 26-Apr-2023 [R3]. aicounsel Substack cites an SEC **Form D dated Apr 25, 2023 for a $25.83M equity offering** (16 investors; related persons: Gabriel Pereyra, Winston Weinberg [CFO at filing], Pat Grady) [R4]. Headline $21M vs filing-derived ~$25.8–25.9M.
- **Series A valuation:** NOT disclosed in any primary source found (Harvey blog and Sequoia post give no valuation). The task's hypothesized "~$150M" is unconfirmed by any source located — GAP.
- **Series B:** Harvey says **$80M / Dec 19, 2023 / $715M** [R1]. PitchBook lists Series B at **$77M**, raised-to-date $103M, dated 12-Jan-2024 [R3]. Tracxn dates it Dec 21, 2023 [R3].
- **Series E valuation:** Harvey states **$5B** [R1]. Manhattan Venture Partners describes it as a "$300M Series E **extension** round in June 2025 at a **pre-money valuation of $9.2B (post-money $9.3B)**" [R3] — internally consistent ($9.0B pre + $0.3B) but flatly contradicts Harvey's $5B; likely an MVP data error or conflation, flagged not resolved. PitchBook dates Series E 19-May-2025 [R3] vs Harvey's Jun 23, 2025 announcement.
- **EQT round:** Harvey's Series F blog confirms EQT invested ("The round follows EQT's recent investment") but gives no amount and did not announce it as a standalone Harvey round [R1]. SiliconANGLE sizes it €50M ≈ $58M [R3]; Tracxn logs $58.7M on Oct 06, 2025 and tags it "Series E" at $5B [R3]. So the €50M/$58.7M figure originates from press/aggregators, not Harvey.
- **Series F date:** Closed ~late Oct 2025 (Tracxn Oct 29, 2025 [R3]; TechCrunch "valued at $8 billion... in late October" [R2]) but announced Dec 4, 2025 by Harvey [R1]. Tracxn also carries an extra "Nov 27, 2025 — Undisclosed — Series F" line [R3].
- **Growth Round naming:** Harvey/CNBC/TechCrunch call it a "Growth Round" [R1/R2]; Tracxn labels it "**Series G**" and PitchBook "Later Stage VC" [R3]. Date Mar 25, 2026 (Harvey) vs Mar 24, 2026 (Tracxn).
- **Conviction / SV Angel first appearance:** Harvey's own Series A blog names **Conviction and SV Angel at Series A** [R1]; Tracxn says Conviction first invested at Series D (Feb 2025) and SV Angel at Series C (Jul 2024) [R3]. Harvey primary places both earlier.
- **Sequoia at seed:** revenuememo claims "Sequoia participated in the seed round" [R4]; contradicted by Sequoia's own post + Tracxn, which put Sequoia's first investment at the **Series A** (Apr 27, 2023) [R1/R3].
